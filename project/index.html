LAP Introduction

In the Learning Advisory Program (LAP), individual and customized learning support is provided to learners through one-on-one/group sessions (via appointments), group sessions(at live sessions), and/or through written advising. Learners who would like to contact learning advisors may ask for an appointment or simply register for a scheduled session whenever they are available.

What kind of support can you get?

We offer learner support and guidance mainly in the following areas:

‣ Identifying learner-specific problems
‣ Dealing with complicated situations related to learning
‣ Dealing with stress and anxiety
‣ Ways to increase motivation
‣ Goal-setting
‣ Making a learning plan
‣ Deciding on the most appropriate learning strategies and techniques for each learner
‣ Time-management
‣ Recommending learning resources suitable for individual needs

It is important to keep in mind that the skills and learning strategies mentioned above are not only related to language learning, but they are necessary skills that one needs to gain for lifelong learning as well.
