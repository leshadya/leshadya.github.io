What do we do in a session?

Our PA-led LAP club sessions are designed to be engaging, interactive, and, most importantly, tailored to your needs as a language learner. Led by our experienced PLA (Peer Learning Advisor) team, you'll have the chance to practice English and learn how to learn in a supportive environment. To get started, fill in the form for the sessions. Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to enhance your language-learning journey! We look forward to seeing you at our upcoming sessions!
