AYBU LAP aspires to build a collaborative, inclusive community that promotes resilience and well-being while preparing learners for long-term success. Our main objectives are (1) to help learners improve their language skills and (2) to let learners think and reflect on their learning environment and thus make them aware of their feelings when learning. AYBU LAP proudly presents its very first blog named Advising Diaries. It has been designed and built by our Peer Advisor Team from scratch. Our Peer Advisors, learners from various departments of AYBU, are trained to gain advising skills to conduct intentional dialogues with other learners to help them learn about and improve themselves as learners and discover new ways of thinking and learning. We hope you will enjoy our precious butterfly reflections and flutter with us on our Advising Diaries.
